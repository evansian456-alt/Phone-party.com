Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Phone Party.

Phone Party uses a real-time sync engine to lock all connected phones to the same audio timestamp. The host uploads music or connects a streaming provider, and every phone in the party — connected via the party code — plays the audio in perfect sync. Think of it like turning every phone speaker into one element of a giant surround sound system.
Free parties support up to 2 phones. With a Party Pass (£3.99) you can sync up to 4 phones for a 2-hour session. Pro Monthly (£9.99/month) scales up to 10 phones with unlimited party time. Our sync engine is built to handle it all — whether it's 2 people or 10, it stays tight.
Absolutely. You can upload MP3, WAV, FLAC, and AAC files directly from your phone. Uploaded tracks deliver the best sync quality since there's no streaming latency. Files are distributed to all guest phones automatically once uploaded to the party.
Yes! You can connect Spotify Premium through our streaming integration. Additionally, our new Official App Sync (beta) lets you play Spotify normally on your device while Phone Party automatically syncs every other phone in the room to your stream. Spotify Premium is required for the direct integration.
Party Pass is a one-time, per-party upgrade (£3.99) that gives you up to 4 phones on sync for a 2-hour session. You get chat and emoji reactions, guest quick replies, DJ quick message buttons, auto party prompts, Official App Sync, and no ads. It's designed for small parties where you want the full experience for one night without a subscription.
Official App Sync is our breakthrough feature (currently in beta) that lets you use any streaming app — Spotify, YouTube Music, Apple Music — on your own device, and Phone Party will detect the audio and automatically sync all guest phones to match. You don't need to use our in-app music player at all. Your library, your taste, your party.
